Why come to Bolo World Gym?

Personal Training

Train like an athlete with our personal trainers at hand to help you achieve your desirable goals. The greatest champions of our lifetime also had personal trainers by their side, everyone needs a push here and there to get the best out of themselves. Whatever your goals are and ambitions are, our dedicated team of personal trainers will help you by providing you with courage, nutrition plans, weekly progress reviews and much more.

State Of The Art

At our gym we have state of the art equipment, all the top gear that you will need to work every muscle group from upper body down to lower body. Whether that may be compound exercises or isolation exercises we have got it all. We even have a dedicated calisthenic area and big studio for class workouts. Come and book a gym induction tour with one of our assistants to see around for yourself.

Fitness Classes

The classes available are yoga for those wanting a more relaxed workout as yoga builds your strength, stability, breathing, flexing and mental well-being. Body combat for those looking for something different as it involves shadow kickboxing and pad work, so work on those self-defence skills burning up to 800+ a session! And our popular hit class focusing on high intensity exercise to burn 500+ calories a session!

Spa Therapy

For those looking for a more relaxed time join our state of the art spa therapy area. This includes a swimming pool, wide range of saunas and steam rooms, a jacuzzi. We also have a massage room with a wide range of massages available at your request from sport massages to Swedish massages and so on.
